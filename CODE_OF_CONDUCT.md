# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the *Sovereign Spine*: govern first,
speak second.

- Be precise.
- Be kind.
- Do not claim authority you do not hold.
- Do not weaken evidence labels for marketing.
- Disagreement is welcome; bypassing governance is not.

Violations may result in PRs being closed, comments being removed, or
contributors being asked to leave the project. The Architect (J.G.A.)
has final say.
