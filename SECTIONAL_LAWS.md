# Sectional Laws

## Silence Coat Purpose

The Silence Coat is the first public shield. Its role is external signal
nullification: dampen unmanaged noise, reject uncontrolled ingress, and
prepare only stable input for the wall.

## Membrane Wall

The Membrane Wall handles context encoding. It translates public-facing
signals into bounded, inspectable context so the braid system can reason
without exposing the spine.

## Cubic Trapping Scales

The Cubic Trapping Scales hold trusted garbage logic. They contain raw
inference, isolate discardable material, and release only what survives
verification.
