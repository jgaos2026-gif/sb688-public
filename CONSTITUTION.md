# Constitution

## Immutable laws (public version)

### 1. Perinatal Brick Immunity

The Perinatal Brick is the root law of SB689 v3. It is treated as the
immutable origin of public governance, and no outer layer may override
or directly mutate it.

### 2. Triple Braid Data Verification

No data is admitted as trusted until it passes through triple-braid
verification paths that converge without contradiction. Material that
cannot be confirmed is contained, varied for study, or purged.

### 3. Spine Proxy Intermediary

Nothing touches the spine directly. The spine accepts only verified
proxy commands relayed through the braid and never direct external
signals.
